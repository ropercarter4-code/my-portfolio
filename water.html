Water Usage

Data centers require large amounts of water to cool their servers and prevent overheating. As technology grows and AI systems demand more computing power, many facilities are consuming millions of gallons of water each year. This can create serious concerns for communities dealing with droughts or limited water resources.

Major Water Concerns

  • Cooling systems require massive amounts of water
  • Some facilities use millions of gallons annually
  • Can worsen drought conditions in certain states
  • Local communities may lose access to water resources
  • Water waste increases as AI demand grows
