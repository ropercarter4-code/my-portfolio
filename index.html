Data Centers: The Hidden Costs of Our Digital World

Data Centers are becoming essential for our digital world to grow. From cloud storage to every piece of social media you see, there is a data center behind it. There are a number of benefits which I will cover in this website, but there is also a completely hidden side of data centers that might shock you. Including water usage, power usage, and land displacement for many communities. This website explores both the benefits of data centers and the hidden environmental costs that are often overlooked.
